    Tips

  • When going into a popular clothing store like Forever21, try to go in the beginning of the month.

  • If you are on a budget go to the sale rack first.

  • Never mix colors that are similar such as, red & orange or gold and silver,also don't mix patterns.

  • Make sure your shoes match your outfit.

  • Always try on your clothes before buying them.
